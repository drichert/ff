SHIPPING




I bring irises to the iris.  Spanned, died, bridged, field, high, low.  Hidden rest, strife, the face, bare, copy, sweet earth.  Controlled, fabled, found, chain, control, blank, a key, beam, banter, a flare, a dream.  Sparks, blossoming, pale, arc, chameleon, the soul, the eye, the artist’s brain.  Following, pausing, standing, out of reach, a chord, a chord, a canvas, a lure, a portal, a helmet, a head.  I give.  I beckon.  Cool repose.  I bear.  I hope to see the birth of gentler children. I
